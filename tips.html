Tips for brewing at home

Use fresh, whole beans

Freshly roasted coffee beans
help give the best flavor

Measure with a scale

Weighing coffee is more accurate than scoops for consistency

Right coffee-to-water ratio

Adjust the ratio to balance strength, sweetness, and body

Match grind to method

Match grind size to brew method to avoid sourness or bitterness

Use filtered water

Filtered water makes a cleaner, smoother cup

Keep experimenting

Try new beans, ratios, and brews to learn
