Caffè Latte

How to make a caffè latte

Ingredients:
1 shot espresso
6 oz steamed milk
1 oz milk foam


Instructions:
Pull an espresso shot.
Steam milk and pour it over the espresso.
Top with a thin layer of microfoam and enjoy.
